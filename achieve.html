ACHIEVEMENTS


The group's debut single album 2 Cool 4 Skool (2013) despite achieving little commercial
success.They released their second EP Skool Luv Affair (2014) and first studio album Dark &Wild
(2014)the following year, to a quieter awards reception; Skool Luv Affair's lead single,"Boy in Luv",
received a Pt. 1 (2015), won the Disc Bonsang at the 2016 Golden Disc Awards, while its lead single
"I Need U" received the Best Male Dance award at the 2015 Melon Music Awards. The album's second
single, "Fire", received a Best Dance – Male nomination at the 2016 Melon Music Awards.In March
2022, BTS became the most-awarded foreign artist in the history of the Japan Gold Disc Awards when
they won 10 awards—this included a record fourth consecutive Artist of the Year (Asia)—at the 36th
ceremony, taking their total to 30 overall. They are the only foreign artist to achieve as many consecutive wins in the category and to receive as many awards in a single ceremony.
